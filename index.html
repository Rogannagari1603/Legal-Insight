Project Idea

PaperHub

In today's world, the rapid increase in published papers makes it challenging for users to find relevant research. We propose a project focused on developing a research paper recommender system using a hybrid approach. The system recommends similar papers based on research topics and keywords, utilizing big data tools for data ingestion and processing to significantly reduce query times. The goal is to enhance the efficiency and accuracy of recommending relevant research papers to users, leveraging both content-based and collaborative filtering techniques.â€‹
